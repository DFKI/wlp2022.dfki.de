--- category: news title: Submission Deadline Extended layout: news permalink: /news/2022-06-19-deadline-extension/ ---

The submission deadline for papers of the 36th Workshop on (Constraint) Logic Programming has been extended by two weeks. Papers must now be submitted on July 4. We look forward to your submissions.

Please note that the EasyChair conference management system (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ki2022) indicates the deadlines of the KI2022 main event (May 16 and 23). Submission for workshops is possible nevertheless. Please do so by selecting track W9: 36th Workshop on (Constraint) Logic Programming.
